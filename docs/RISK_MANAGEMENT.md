# Risk Management Rules

## Position Sizing
- **Max per trade:** 5% of portfolio ($1,500 on $30K)
- **Max per ticker:** 10% of portfolio ($3,000 on $30K)
- **Max total CSP exposure:** 50% of portfolio
- **Cash reserve:** Always maintain 20% minimum

## Entry Criteria (ALL must be met)
1. ✅ S&P 500 constituent OR approved watchlist ticker
2. ✅ No earnings within 7 days
3. ✅ 10% or more OTM strike
4. ✅ Delta between 0.20 and 0.35
5. ✅ 7-14 days to expiration
6. ✅ Weekly yield ≥ 0.7%
7. ✅ Open interest ≥ 200 contracts
8. ✅ Bid-ask spread ≤ 20% of premium

## Exit Rules
- **Profit target:** 50% of max profit (close at 50% gain)
- **Time stop:** Close at 21 DTE regardless of profit
- **Stop loss:** Never let a position go ITM without a plan
- **Rolling:** Roll out and down if tested, never add to losers

## Portfolio Protection
- If VIX > 30: Reduce position sizes by 50%
- If VIX > 40: Stop all new trades, manage existing only
- If portfolio down >5% in a week: Review and pause new trades

## Forbidden Actions
- ❌ No naked calls (unlimited risk)
- ❌ No earnings plays
- ❌ No meme stocks or highly volatile tickers
- ❌ No averaging down on losing positions
- ❌ No FOMO trades outside of scan criteria

## Monitoring
- Check open positions daily
- Review P&L weekly
- Stress test monthly
- Adjust rules quarterly based on performance

---

**Remember:** Preservation of capital comes first. Profits second.
